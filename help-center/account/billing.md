# Billing & Plans

## Plans

WorkflowFiesta offers plans for individuals, teams, and enterprises. All plans include the core platform — agents, skills, workflows, and credentials.

| Plan | Best for |
|------|----------|
| **Free** | Individuals exploring the platform |
| **Pro** | Small teams running regular automations |
| **Enterprise** | Organizations with advanced security, compliance, and scale needs |

To see current pricing and plan details, visit [workflowfiesta.com/pricing](https://workflowfiesta.com/pricing) or ask WorkflowFiesta:
> *"What plan am I on and what are my limits?"*

## AI model costs

WorkflowFiesta connects to AI model providers using your own API keys (Bring Your Own Model). Model usage costs are billed directly by the provider — OpenAI, Anthropic, AWS, etc. — not by WorkflowFiesta.

WorkflowFiesta gives you full visibility into model spend and lets you set limits to control costs.

## Spend limits

You can set hard spend limits at any level — org-wide, per user, per workflow, or per agent. When a limit is reached, runs are paused until the period resets or the limit is raised.

To set a limit:
> *"Set a monthly AI spend limit of $100 for my org."*
> *"Limit the weekly report workflow to $5 per run."*

## Viewing usage and costs

You can see a full cost breakdown at any time:
> *"How much have we spent on AI this month?"*
> *"Which workflow is costing the most?"*
> *"Show me a cost breakdown by model."*

WorkflowFiesta will pull your usage data and show you a ranked breakdown.

## Upgrading or changing your plan

To upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan, go to **Settings → Billing** or ask WorkflowFiesta:
> *"I want to upgrade to Pro."*

## Invoices and receipts

Invoices are available in **Settings → Billing → Invoice history**. They are also emailed to the billing contact on your account after each billing cycle.
