# Billing & Plans

## Plans

WorkflowFiesta is a paid platform. There is no free tier.

| Plan           | Price                 | Best for                                                              |
| -------------- | --------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Pro**        | $100 per user / month | Teams running AI agents and automated workflows                       |
| **Enterprise** | Custom pricing        | Organizations with advanced security, compliance, SSO, or scale needs |

Every plan includes full platform access — agents, skills, workflows, credentials, the runner, and all integrations. There are no feature-gated tiers.

To see current pricing or discuss Enterprise, visit [workflowfiesta.com/pricing](https://workflowfiesta.com/pricing) or ask WorkflowFiesta directly:

> _"What plan am I on?"_

***

## AI model costs

WorkflowFiesta connects to AI model providers using your own API keys. **Model usage costs are billed directly by the provider** — Anthropic, OpenAI, AWS, Google, etc. — not by WorkflowFiesta.

This means:

* WorkflowFiesta never marks up model costs
* You pay providers at their published rates
* You can switch providers at any time to control costs
* You have full visibility into exactly what each agent, workflow, and user is spending

{% hint style="info" %}
**Bring Your Own Model (BYOM)** — WorkflowFiesta works with any AI provider. Your API keys are stored encrypted and used only for your runs.
{% endhint %}

***

## Token spend and cost visibility

Every agent run, workflow step, and conversation consumes AI tokens. WorkflowFiesta tracks all of this and gives you a full cost breakdown at any time.

Ask WorkflowFiesta:

> _"How much have we spent on AI this month?"_ _"Which workflow is costing the most?"_ _"Show me a cost breakdown by model."_ _"Which user is spending the most on AI?"_

WorkflowFiesta will pull your usage data and return a ranked breakdown by model, workflow, agent, or user — for the last 7, 30, or 90 days.

***

## Spend limits

You can set hard spend limits at any level to prevent unexpected costs. When a limit is reached, runs are paused until the period resets or an Admin raises the limit.

**Limit scopes:**

| Scope        | Example                                       |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------- |
| Org-wide     | Total AI spend across all users and workflows |
| Per user     | Limit how much any individual can spend       |
| Per workflow | Cap the cost of a specific automated pipeline |
| Per agent    | Limit spend on a specific agent               |

**Limit periods:** Daily · Weekly · Monthly

To set a limit, ask WorkflowFiesta:

> _"Set a monthly AI spend limit of $500 for my org."_ _"Limit the weekly report workflow to $10 per run."_ _"Set a $50 monthly limit for the marketing team's agents."_

***

## Invoices and receipts

Invoices are available in **Settings → Billing → Invoice history**. They are also emailed to the billing contact on your account after each billing cycle.

To update your billing contact, payment method, or billing address, go to **Settings → Billing**.

***

## Upgrading, downgrading, or cancelling

To change your plan or cancel, go to **Settings → Billing** or contact the team via [Discord](https://discord.gg/XEKxARDkNQ).

{% hint style="warning" %}
Cancelling your plan will immediately revoke access for all users in your organization. Agents, workflows, and credentials are retained for 30 days in case you reactivate.
{% endhint %}

***
