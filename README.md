## What You Can Do

**Build AI agents through conversation**
Describe what you want an agent to do in plain English. WorkflowFiesta configures it — no code, no YAML, no prompt engineering required.

**Automate multi-step workflows**
Chain agents, scripts, and HTTP calls into workflows that run on a schedule, respond to a webhook, or trigger from a chat message.

**Connect to your local systems**
Install the WorkflowFiesta Runner on your machine or server. Agents can then read local files, query internal databases, and run tools that live behind your firewall.

**Bring your own AI model**
Connect OpenAI, Anthropic, AWS Bedrock, or any compatible provider. Switch models at any time — no lock-in, no migration cost.

## Who It's For

**Operations teams** who want automation without waiting on engineering. If you can describe a process in words, you can automate it in WorkflowFiesta.

**Business leaders** who want AI working across departments — not just in a chat window, but actually completing tasks, sending reports, and managing workflows end to end.

**Technical teams** who want a production-ready platform. Skip the infrastructure work and focus on building the agents and workflows your business actually needs.
