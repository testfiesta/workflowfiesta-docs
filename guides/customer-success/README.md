## What Customer Success teams automate

### Health monitoring

Track customer engagement signals and surface accounts that need attention before they churn.

> _"Create a weekly workflow that reviews our customer activity data, flags any accounts with declining engagement, and sends me a prioritized list every Monday morning."_

### Renewal alerts

Get notified about upcoming renewals with enough lead time to act.

> _"Alert me 90, 60, and 30 days before any customer contract renewal. Include their current plan, usage, and last contact date."_

### QBR preparation

Generate quarterly business review decks and talking points from customer data.

> _"Prepare a QBR summary for Acme Corp. Pull their usage stats, key wins from the last quarter, and suggest 3 expansion opportunities."_

### Churn signals

Identify at-risk accounts based on usage patterns, support tickets, and engagement drops.

> _"Which of our customers haven't logged in for 30 days? Rank them by contract value and flag anyone up for renewal in the next 60 days."_

### Customer communications

Draft personalized check-in emails, renewal outreach, and expansion proposals.

> _"Write a check-in email to a customer who hasn't used the product in 3 weeks. Warm tone, offer a quick call, mention a new feature they'd find useful."_
