Automate job descriptions, onboarding, policy drafts, and people operations so your HR team focuses on people, not paperwork.

***

## What HR & People teams automate

### Job descriptions

Generate complete, well-structured job descriptions from a brief conversation about the role.

> _"Write a job description for a Senior Product Manager. We're a B2B SaaS company, Series B, remote-first. Focus on ownership and cross-functional collaboration."_

### Onboarding checklists

Create personalized onboarding plans for new hires based on their role, team, and start date.

> _"Create an onboarding checklist for a new marketing hire starting Monday. Include tools to set up, people to meet, and 30/60/90 day goals."_

### Policy drafts

Draft HR policies, employee handbooks, and process documentation in plain language.

> _"Draft a remote work policy for our company. We're fully remote, async-first, with team members in 6 time zones."_

### Org announcements

Write clear, professional internal communications for team changes, policy updates, and company news.

> _"Write an announcement for our all-hands about our new parental leave policy. Warm tone, clear on the details, no corporate speak."_

### Interview guides

Generate structured interview guides with role-specific questions and evaluation criteria.

> _"Create an interview guide for a Customer Success Manager role. Include behavioral questions, a scoring rubric, and red flags to watch for."_
